## Phase 1: Mathematical Foundations (2-3 months)

### 📐 Linear Algebra

#### Detailed Syllabus:
1. **Vectors and Vector Spaces**
   - Vector operations, dot product, cross product
   - Linear independence, basis, span
   - Vector norms (L1, L2, infinity norm)

2. **Matrices and Matrix Operations**
   - Matrix multiplication, transpose, inverse
   - Determinants and their properties
   - Matrix rank and null space

3. **Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors**
   - Characteristic polynomial
   - Diagonalization
   - Applications in PCA and dimensionality reduction

4. **Matrix Decompositions**
   - LU decomposition
   - QR decomposition
   - Singular Value Decomposition (SVD)
   - Principal Component Analysis (PCA)

### 📖 Must-Read Foundation Papers

#### Machine Learning Classics
1. **"A Few Useful Things to Know About Machine Learning"** - Pedro Domingos (2012)
   - **Why**: Practical wisdom for ML practitioners
   - **Key Insights**: No free lunch theorem, feature engineering importance

2. **"The Elements of Statistical Learning"** - Hastie, Tibshirani, Friedman
   - **Why**: Comprehensive statistical learning theory
   - **Best For**: Mathematical foundations

#### Deep Learning Foundations
1. **"Deep Learning"** - LeCun, Bengio, Hinton (Nature, 2015)
   - **Why**: Overview by the three godfathers of deep learning
   - **Key Insights**: Representation learning, end-to-end learning

2. **"Gradient-Based Learning Applied to Document Recognition"** - LeCun et al. (1998)
   - **Why**: Introduction of CNNs and LeNet
   - **Historical Importance**: Foundation of modern computer vision

### 🔥 Breakthrough Papers by Year

#### 2017: The Transformer Revolution
1. **"Attention Is All You Need"** - Vaswani et al.
   - **Impact**: Revolutionized NLP and beyond
   - **Key Innovation**: Self-attention mechanism
   - **Applications**: BERT, GPT, Vision Transformers

#### 2018: BERT Era
1. **"BERT: Pre-training of Deep Bidirectional Transformers"** - Devlin et al.
   - **Impact**: New SOTA on 11 NLP tasks
   - **Key Innovation**: Bidirectional context understanding

#### 2019: GPT-2 and Scaling
1. **"Language Models are Unsupervised Multitask Learners"** - Radford et al.
   - **Impact**: Demonstrated scaling benefits
   - **Key Innovation**: Zero-shot task transfer

#### 2020: GPT-3 and Few-Shot Learning
1. **"Language Models are Few-Shot Learners"** - Brown et al.
   - **Impact**: 175B parameters, emergent abilities
   - **Key Innovation**: In-context learning

#### 2021: Vision Transformers
1. **"An Image is Worth 16x16 Words"** - Dosovitskiy et al.
   - **Impact**: Transformers beat CNNs on ImageNet
   - **Key Innovation**: Pure attention for vision

#### 2022: ChatGPT and RLHF
1. **"Training language models to follow instructions with human feedback"** - Ouyang et al.
   - **Impact**: Foundation for ChatGPT
   - **Key Innovation**: RLHF for alignment
