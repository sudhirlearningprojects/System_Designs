# Amazon Interview Study Plans

## 🎯 30-Day Intensive Plan

### Week 1: Arrays & Strings (Days 1-7)
**Goal**: Master sliding window, two pointers, hash map patterns

**Day 1-2**: Easy Problems (10 problems)
- Two Sum, Valid Palindrome, Valid Anagram, Best Time to Buy Stock
- Move Zeroes, Contains Duplicate, Reverse String, Plus One
- Merge Sorted Array, Remove Duplicates

**Day 3-4**: Medium Problems (10 problems)
- Longest Substring Without Repeating, 3Sum, Product Except Self
- Container With Most Water, Group Anagrams, Subarray Sum Equals K
- Merge Intervals, Longest Palindromic Substring, Find All Anagrams
- Next Permutation

**Day 5-6**: Hard Problems (5 problems)
- Minimum Window Substring, Trapping Rain Water, First Missing Positive
- Sliding Window Maximum, Median of Two Sorted Arrays

**Day 7**: Review & Mock Interview
- Solve 3 random problems (1 easy, 1 medium, 1 hard)
- Time yourself: Easy 15min, Medium 30min, Hard 45min

---

### Week 2: Trees & Graphs (Days 8-14)

**Day 8-9**: Tree Basics (10 problems)
- Max Depth, Invert Tree, Diameter, Balanced Tree
- Level Order Traversal, Zigzag Level Order, Right Side View
- Path Sum, Binary Tree Paths, Symmetric Tree

**Day 10-11**: BST & Advanced Trees (10 problems)
- Validate BST, Kth Smallest in BST, LCA, Serialize/Deserialize
- Construct from Preorder/Inorder, Flatten to Linked List
- Binary Tree Max Path Sum, Count Complete Nodes
- All Nodes Distance K, BST Iterator

**Day 12-13**: Graphs (10 problems)
- Number of Islands, Clone Graph, Course Schedule I & II
- Word Ladder, Pacific Atlantic, Graph Valid Tree
- Alien Dictionary, Network Delay Time, Accounts Merge

**Day 14**: Review & Mock Interview

---

### Week 3: DP & Linked Lists (Days 15-21)

**Day 15-16**: DP Basics (10 problems)
- Climbing Stairs, House Robber I & II, Coin Change
- Word Break, Longest Palindromic Substring, Unique Paths
- Maximum Subarray, Maximum Product Subarray, Decode Ways

**Day 17-18**: Advanced DP (10 problems)
- LIS, LCS, Edit Distance, Partition Equal Subset Sum
- Best Time to Buy Stock III, Target Sum, Burst Balloons
- Longest Palindromic Subsequence, Regular Expression Matching
- Wildcard Matching

**Day 19-20**: Linked Lists (10 problems)
- Reverse Linked List, Merge Two Lists, Merge K Lists
- Add Two Numbers, Linked List Cycle, Copy Random Pointer
- Reorder List, Remove Nth Node, Sort List, Reverse K Group

**Day 21**: Review & Mock Interview

---

### Week 4: Design, Heap & Final Prep (Days 22-30)

**Day 22-23**: Stack & Queue (10 problems)
- Valid Parentheses, Min Stack, Daily Temperatures
- Largest Rectangle, Evaluate RPN, Basic Calculator II
- Sliding Window Maximum, Queue using Stacks, Decode String
- Trapping Rain Water

**Day 24-25**: Heap & Greedy (10 problems)
- Top K Frequent, Median from Stream, K Closest Points
- Task Scheduler, Meeting Rooms II, Reorganize String
- Merge K Lists, Kth Largest, Jump Game I & II, Gas Station

**Day 26-27**: Design Problems (8 problems)
- LRU Cache, Design HashMap, Implement Trie
- Design Twitter, Min Stack, Max Stack
- Design Hit Counter, Time Based Key-Value Store

**Day 28**: Backtracking (8 problems)
- Permutations, Subsets, Combination Sum, Generate Parentheses
- Word Search, Palindrome Partitioning, N-Queens
- Letter Combinations

**Day 29-30**: Mock Interviews & Review
- 3 full mock interviews (2 coding + 1 behavioral)
- Review all patterns and templates
- Practice explaining solutions aloud

## 🔥 Pro Tips

1. **Consistency > Intensity**: 2 hours daily beats 10 hours on weekends
2. **Understand, Don't Memorize**: Focus on patterns, not solutions
3. **Time Yourself**: Always practice with timer
4. **Mock Interviews**: Do at least 1 per week
5. **Review Mistakes**: Spend 30 min daily reviewing wrong answers
6. **Explain Aloud**: Practice explaining your thought process
7. **Use Debugger**: Step through code to understand flow
8. **Join Study Groups**: Discuss problems with peers
9. **Track Progress**: Maintain a spreadsheet of solved problems
10. **Stay Healthy**: Exercise, sleep well, eat healthy
